Ribeye Steak

Description

Salting the ribeye steak two days prior to cooking brines the steak, helps tenderize it, and adds rich flavor.

Ingredients

  • 6 (8 ounce) rib-eye steaks
  • 1 tablespoon Diamond CrystalÂ® Kosher Salt
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil, or as needed
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter

Steps

  1. Mix ingredients with meat two days prior
  2. Take steak out of the fridge 30 mins before grilling
  3. Heat a cast iron pan to smoking hot
  4. Cook each side for 3-4 mins
  5. Eat up!
